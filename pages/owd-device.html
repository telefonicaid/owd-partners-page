OWD hardware

A lot of power in a small package

Really powerful device

Cheap doesn't mean slow or bad. OWD first device will have much of the hardware big devices have, like GPS, FM Radio, Bluetooth and touch keys.

But apart from that, there is a huge opportunity to build great apps with little effort for this device. It's really powerful, it has everything a phone should have and still open for more from you!

Hardware

You should not target any specific hardware for your apps, but if you want to know what cool hardware will have the first OWD, you should look at this list!
